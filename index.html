Game Log

A web app that allows users to signup and create their own library to track video games they have played or want to play and leave their own personal ratings, reviews, progress, and thoughts about the game.

GameLog is built on a Ruby on Rails backend, Vue.js frontend, database managed by PostgreSQL, themed using CSS framework Bootstrap, and game information pulled from third party API, RAWG.

About me

Picture of the Maldive above, a bucket list destination I have yet to check off.

Hello! My name is Jason, I am an anime lover, video gamer, technology enjoyer, and software developer.

My journey into software development began after the completion of my Sonography Associate's Degree, where after wrapping up a year of clinical experience, I came to the realization that I no longer want to pursue a career I have come to dread.

The realization led me down a path of discovery and self reflection to figure out what I am actually passionate about. That passion has always been technology related, whether it be tech integration into video games or my new iPhone. My research into tech related careers brought me to programming. Just to make sure this venture does not end up like the last, I purchased a python class on Udemy and in which I discovered that I enjoy debugging and creative problem solving. However, I got frustrated with the structure, feeling it was not an environment conducive to learning for a beginner programmer like myself. I enrolled myself into the Actualize bootcamp where I am able to benefit from the help of a live instructor and cohort to further develop my skills to continue my software development journey.
